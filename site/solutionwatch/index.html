Seeing the pot is step one. This page is for step two — what you can actually do about it.

SolutionWatch will list vetted services, tools, and providers that help individuals reduce their exposure to state surveillance and control — privacy tooling, alternative banking and residency options, and similar. It is being built deliberately slowly, because a page that recommends real products people might pay real money for needs a real vetting standard first, not just a partner willing to sign an affiliate agreement.

ⓘ Affiliate disclosure, in advance: some listings on this page, once published, will be affiliate partnerships — we may earn a commission if you use a partner's service through a link here, at no extra cost to you. That compensation will never influence what's published on the Composite Index or Primary Questions pages, and it won't influence which countries a listed provider is shown to — see the separation policy below. A full, public Partner & Affiliate Policy (vetting criteria, disclosure standard, revenue transparency) will be published here before any partner listing goes live.

Why this page is structurally separate

SolutionWatch lives at its own address, uses its own layout, and nothing on the Composite Index or Primary Questions pages links here automatically — no country's score page suggests a "solution," and nothing here references a specific country's score. That's deliberate: this project's credibility depends on its data being impartial, and the clearest way to keep a monetized recommendations page from quietly warping that is to keep them structurally apart, not just verbally promise they're unrelated. See docs/methodology.md for how the data itself is sourced and scored.
